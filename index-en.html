What is FOMUS

Making Japanese culture beloved around the world.

Rooted in the traditional Japanese craft of masu, FOMUS is a brand that brings the value of Japanese culture to the world through craft, technology, entertainment, and community.

What is Masu?

The masu began as a measuring vessel and evolved into a symbol of good fortune and celebration, woven into the rhythms and milestones of Japanese life.

At FOMUS, we have reinterpreted the masu's meaning — transforming it into a communication tool that bridges culture and the world through the power of technology and community.
